Meet Our Team Of Experts


Founder & CEO
Josiah Precious Anuoluwapo

Josiah Precious, an African serial entrepreneur and investor based in Dubai, has been actively involved in the web3 industry since 2020. With over 4 years of experience in the marketing industry, Precious has successfully launched a web3 and tech-related talk show that promotes the adoption of blockchain and web3 technologies. While she has explored various industries, her true passion lies in the crypto and blockchain space, which she discovered in 2020 through her experience as a crypto trader. Precious has collaborated with numerous crypto and web3 brands as an influencer, forging partnerships and advocating for their adoption.
Driven by her dedication to the blockchain industry, Precious aims to facilitate mass adoption through various channels such as marketing, press, social media, and educational platforms. Recognizing the potential of the crypto and blockchain space, she has taken on the role of a tutor, sharing her knowledge and opportunities with hundreds of individuals seeking to engage in this exciting industry. As her expertise grew, she began working as an advisor and influencer for prominent companies within the crypto space. Eventually, Precious established her own Crypto & Web3 marketing agency and PR firm.
Passionate about educating others, Precious frequently speaks at conferences in Africa and even travels globally to share her insights and knowledge about blockchain technology. Her personal growth within the industry has been remarkable, and she aspires to make a broader impact on a global scale.
